About Us

PSG Centre for Sponsored Research and Consultancy was established in 1989 in order to foster industrial consultancy and research. Consultancy work and testing of materials / products are being done for several Government and private industries and organisations by all the departments of the college through the centre.

  • The Consultancy, Testing and Research activities carried out by the various Departments and Centres are detailed in this booklet
  • Industries and Organisations desirous of making use of the facilities of the college for their requirements may contact...
  • Head of Department or Centre/ Principal,PSG college of technology/ Dean, PSG Centre for Sponsored Research and Consultancy

All the Departments and Centres of the PSG College of Technology are carrying out Research, Consultancy and Testing activities ever since the college was established. PSG Tech is a very good example for Industry-Institute Interaction with a Manufacturing Industry, PSG Industrial Institute located in the same campus. PSG Tech offers a unique Sandwich Degree programme in which the students have course work as well as Industrial Training exposure side by side paralley every day.

Industrial Associateship

  • 01Industrial Associateship

    Registered Manufacturing & Processing Industries, Organisations and Government Agencies are eligible to become Industrial Associates of PSG College of Technology. The objectives of the Industrial Associateship scheme are to reorient the educational structure to suit the demand of the industry and also to disseminate the latest developments in the institutions to the Industries.

  • 02Special Facilities offered for Industrial Associates include:
    1. Use of books from the Library. Books can be borrowed paying library deposit of Rs. 2,000/- per book, upto a maximum of 4 books.
    2. Concession of 10% in Testing charges.
    3. Arrangement of special training programmes for specific requirements of associates, either in the premises of the industry or in the College.
    4. In addition to faculty consultancy work, industrial consultancy projects can be undertaken.
    5. Provision for prototype fabrication and testing facilities for product development.
  • 03Industrial Associate Membership Fee :
    1. Small Scale Industry : Rs. 2000/- per year
    2. Medium Scale Industry : Rs. 4000/- per year
    3. Large Scale Industry : Rs. 6000/- per year
